Why Analytics Matter

Without data, you're just guessing. With data, you're making informed decisions.

üìà What to Track

  • User Behavior: What do they click?
  • Engagement: How long do they stay?
  • Conversion: Do they subscribe/donate?
  • Retention: Do they come back?

üéØ Why It Matters

  • Improve UX: Fix confusing parts
  • Increase Revenue: Optimize pricing
  • Grow Users: Find what works
  • Prove Value: Show impact to stakeholders

Real Example

If 1000 people visit your app but only 50 sign up, that's a 5% conversion rate. Analytics help you understand why 95% left and how to improve it!
